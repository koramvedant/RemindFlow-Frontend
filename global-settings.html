Global Settings & Safety

Transparency and trust for your automation

Data Usage

RemindFlow uses invoice and client data only to calculate automation decisions, send reminders, and improve reliability. We never share your data with third parties without consent.

Trust Grades

Client trust grades (A–D) are internal evaluations based on historical payment behavior. They help guide automation and are not guarantees of payment.

Escalation Ethics

Escalation stages and tones are designed to be professional and ethical. RemindFlow will never send aggressive or misleading messages. You can always pause or modify automation globally.
